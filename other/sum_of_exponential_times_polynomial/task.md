## @{keyword.statement}
Given integers $r$, $d$ and $n$, calculate $\sum_{i=0}^{n-1} r^i i^d$ modulo $@{param.MOD}$.
We define $0^0 = 1$.

## @{keyword.constraints}

- $0 \le r < @{param.MOD}$
- $@{param.D_MIN} \le d \le @{param.D_MAX}$
- $@{param.N_MIN} \le n \le @{param.N_MAX}$

## @{keyword.input}

~~~
$r$ $d$ $n$
~~~

## @{keyword.output}

~~~
$\mathit{answer}$
~~~
